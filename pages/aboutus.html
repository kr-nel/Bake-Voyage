About Us

Bake Voyage is the sweet enterprise run by Sherin Abo-abo, Geneva's older sister. What started as a simple love for baking quickly turned into a small business when Sherin realized she could earn money from her delicious creations. Her sister, Geneva, plays a key role by selling the baked goods to her classmates.

Fun fact: The cookies we gave to our Professors on Teacher's Day were actually one of Sherin's delicious creations
